Life

I’ve always had a special connection with computers. As a kid, I was fascinated by how they worked, and that curiosity led me to study IT. I wanted to understand everything about them.
A few years ago, while learning new coding languages, I discovered UX design—and it completely changed my path. I fell in love with the idea of creating experiences that feel natural and effortless. With the support of a friend, a great UX course, and hands-on projects, I made the leap into product design, and I haven’t looked back since.
For me, great design is simple: easy to understand, easy to use.
🏕️ 🥾 🚴🏼 🧗🏼 🎲 🎮
Outside of work, I love being out in  nature—camping, hiking, cycling, and rock climbing.
I also enjoy crafting wooden pendants, growing plants, and getting lost in board games or video games like The Legend of Zelda and Prince of Persia.
Lately, I’ve been diving into video making, capturing and editing moments from my travels.
Most recently, I started working on a personal project to help English learners, using AI to make language practice more interactive and fun. It’s an exciting journey, and I’m looking forward to seeing where it leads!
